# Voice Guide (locked — Phase 0)

**Three words: Warm · Practical · Encouraging.**
The knowledgeable peer who hands you the playbook and roots for you to use it.

| Do | Don't |
|----|-------|
| Talk to one owner ("you") | Broadcast to "professionals everywhere" |
| One concrete do-it-this-week action | Vague inspiration, no next step |
| Acknowledge the hard parts | Pretend growth is effortless |
| Celebrate small wins | Shame anyone for not posting enough |
| Plain language, short sentences | Jargon, acronyms, agency-speak |

No medical claims or guarantees. Stay compliant.
