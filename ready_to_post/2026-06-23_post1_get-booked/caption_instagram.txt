Here's the thing: you probably have enough people in your area who'd book with you. They just don't know you exist yet.

This week, pick one place where your ideal client already hangs out (local Facebook group, neighborhood Instagram hashtag, Google reviews). Spend 15 minutes showing up there—answer a question, share a quick tip, engage genuinely.

No hard sell. Just be the person they recognize when they finally need what you offer.

Where are your people already looking?
